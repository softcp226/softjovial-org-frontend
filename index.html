What is forex trading and how it works?

Forex, also known as foreign exchange or FX is a global decentralized or over-the-counter market for the trading of currencies. This market determines foreign exchange rates for every currency.As the name implies Forex trading is the process of speculating on currency prices to potentially make a profit. Currencies are traded in pairs, so by exchanging one currency for another, a trader is speculating on whether one currency will rise or fall in value against the other.
